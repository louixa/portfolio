LOUISA WONG

I grew up as an artist in New York and found passion for photography after studying film during college. After graduating with a BA in Psychology, photography remained a hobby as I struggled to find my Asian-American identity, switching between a flurry of retail jobs and government-related work. I decided I needed a change and moved to Tokyo in 2019 to attend a 9-week coding bootcamp and gained a life-changing appreciation for web development alongside street photography. Since then I've been working in Tokyo as a freelance photographer and web developer. Outside my work, I enjoy traveling, reading, and experimenting with new forms of photography.
