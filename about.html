Louisa Wong, also known as 'louixa', is a New York-bred freelance photographer currently based in Tokyo, Japan. From what started as a hobby, photography quickly became her main form of expression. Louisa specializes in street, lifestyle, and portrait photography with a strong attention to detail by combining colors, light, composition, and mood to create an aesthetic appeal. Her drive is to create meaningful work that inspires creativity and storytelling.

Outside of photography Louisa is passionate about travel, cultures, and current events. She is always looking to grow and explore new boundaries.
