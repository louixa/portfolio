LOUISA WONG

Born and raised in New York, I grew up as an artist and found passion for photography after picking up a film camera during college. After graduating with a BA in Psychology, photography remained a hobby as I struggled to find my Asian-American identity, switching between a multitude of retail jobs and government-related work. I took a chance in 2019 and moved to Tokyo to attend a 9-week coding bootcamp and gained an appreciation for web development alongside street and travel photography. Since then I've been working in Tokyo as a freelance photographer and web developer. In the end, it doesn't matter where I go, I will always identify as myself doing what I love.
